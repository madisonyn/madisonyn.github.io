Nice to Meet You!


My name is Madison Yn and I'm a 4th year design student with a minor in sustainability in the built environment, graduating in June 2021 from UC Davis. Throughout my design studies, I have come to resonate with UI/UX design and UX research with a human centered approach. As designers, I believe it's important to always evaluate a problem or challenge by developing empathy with your user and understanding their needs. In my work, I'm most excited about fostering connections with my users and learning about their experiences to find human centered solutions for their problems.


Apart from designing, I can be found skate boarding, attempting to land some no-comply combinations. I'm also an avid climber, but quarantine has put my bouldering endeavors on a hiatus for now. In the meantime, I've been investing more hours in baking experiments and discovering new music (specifically of the ambient, bossa nova, house, and folk variety.)
